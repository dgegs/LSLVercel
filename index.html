About Lucky Streak Lounge

Lucky Streak Lounge, Trenton’s go-to spot for great drinks, exciting Video Gaming Terminals (VGTs), and a laid-back atmosphere where everyone feels like a regular. Located in the heart of town at 29 W. Broadway, we’re more than just a bar—we’re a place where good times, good luck, and good company come together.

Whether you're stopping in after work, meeting up with friends, or testing your luck on our state-of-the-art VGTs, Lucky Streak Lounge offers an inviting space to unwind and enjoy. Our gaming area features top-tier machines with big jackpot potential, giving you the thrill of the casino without the long drive.

Lucky Streak Lounge is proudly owned by Kristal Gegelman, Griffin Gegelman, and Doug Gegelman and operated with a passion for bringing people together. As a locally owned business, we value our community and strive to be a place where locals and visitors alike can relax, connect, and enjoy some well-earned fun.

Whether you’re in it for the thrill of the game, a chill night out, or just looking for a friendly face behind the bar, Lucky Streak Lounge is your spot. Swing by, grab a drink, and see if tonight’s your lucky night!
